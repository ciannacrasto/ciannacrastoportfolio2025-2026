Michie's Munchies

Project Brief

The goal of this project was to create a fictional brand and design it’s packaging and create a campaign to sell it. We had options of hoardings, bus branding, posters, videos and more.

concept

Michie’s Munchies was designed to be a cookie brand. It was inspired by my mother’s love for baking and was dedicated to her.
